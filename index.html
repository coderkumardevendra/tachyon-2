How we make things happen

  • Swedish Mindset
    At Tachyon, the Swedish mindset is deeply embedded in the company's DNA. Sweden has long been associated with innovation, sustainability, and a commitment to quality – values that Tachyon wholeheartedly embraces. Just as Sweden is known for its groundbreaking inventions, Tachyon is at the forefront of automotive software innovation. Their team of brilliant engineers and designers constantly pushes boundaries to create cutting-edge solutions that enhance the driving experience and contribute to a sustainable future. Tachyon's Swedish mindset extends to their vision for a sustainable future. They are committed to reducing the automotive industry's environmental impact through software innovation, aligning perfectly with Sweden's eco-conscious approach.
  • Automotive Industry outside of Sweden (AIoS)
    Tachyon Automotive Industry outside of Sweden, refers to the vast and complex network of automobile manufacturers, suppliers, and related businesses that operate worldwide but exclude Sweden from their scope. This industry encompasses a wide range of activities, from designing and producing vehicles to marketing, sales, and aftermarket services. As the AIoS continues to evolve and adapt to new challenges and opportunities, it remains a dynamic and essential component of the global economy. Its contributions to mobility, technological progress, and economic development continue to be a driving force behind our modern way of life.
  • Agile team setups with the end customer
    Tachyon Automotive's Agile team setups and their collaboration with our valued end customers. At Tachyon, we have always prioritized customer satisfaction, and our Agile approach plays a crucial role in ensuring that our software development processes are aligned with our customers' needs and expectations. In conclusion, our Agile team setups at Tachyon Automotive are designed to be customer-centric and adaptive, enabling us to deliver high-quality automotive software that meets and exceeds our customers' expectations. We are committed to maintaining this customer collaboration as a central part of our software development ethos.
  • 99% remote
    A 99% remote work setup suggests that the company primarily relies on employees working from remote locations, such as their homes or co-working spaces, with very few employees working in a physical office.
